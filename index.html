About us

we value your health

Katito Medical Centre is a level 4 hospital with its mother branch in Katito town, which was established in December 2019. We are licensed by Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentist Council, Pharmacy and Poisons Board and Kenya Medical Laboratory Technologists and Technicians Board to operate in Kenya.Our main purpose is to bridge the gap between health care needs of the people and availability of quality affordable healthcare services that is prompt and dependable.

At KMC, we place high regard for safety, professionalism and clear understanding of the patient’s needs which are the hallmark of our culture.
